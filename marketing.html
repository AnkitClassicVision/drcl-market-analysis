Marketing Strategy

Channel mix, messaging framework, and content strategy recommendations

Value Propositions (Priority Order)

Primary Messages

#1 Time Savings = Staff Efficiency

"Reduce CL administration by 50%. 15 minutes to 15 seconds ordering time."

#2 Revenue Recapture

"Keep contact lens sales in-practice. Stop losing patients to 1-800 Contacts and Amazon."

#3 Vendor-Agnostic Freedom

"Order from ANY manufacturer. Not tied to a single distributor."

#4 EMR Integration

"Works with your existing system. RevolutionEHR, Eyefinity, CrystalPM, Officemate."

#5 Built by ODs

"Created by optometrists who understand your practice."

Pain Points to Address

  • Time drain on staff - "10 hours/week on CL administration"
  • Revenue leakage - Patients buying from 1-800 Contacts
  • Multiple vendor complexity - Juggling different portals
  • Manual processes - Duplicate entry, sticky notes
  • Lack of visibility - No CL performance analytics
Key Insight:

Staffing is the #1 pain point. Lead with staff efficiency messaging over revenue capture.
